On the Road2014

iOS App

About Project

- Automatically generate itinerary, making it easier to have an overview of a trip.

- Trip is generated immediately and records are ordered according to time and location.

- You and your friends can record your team trip together.

- Automatically clustering nearby records in a trip.

- Trip gallery slideshow.

- Record with tags including spot, shopping, food, hotel, transport, location, tourist attraction.

- Record your trip and sync them to wechat, weibo, renren, douban.

- Don't worry on mobile fee! you can record your trip even offline. GPS location service is still avaiable when offline.

- Your local timezone is also saved.

- We respect and protect your privacy, you can set your trip and/or record as private, only you can see the trip, or only team members can see the trip.

- Trips and profile info are updated simultaneously .
